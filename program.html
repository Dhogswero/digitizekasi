The Digitize Kasi Program

A comprehensive roadmap from digital exclusion to economic empowerment

Transforming Lives Through Digital Literacy

The Digitize Kasi program is a three-phase, systematic approach to bridging South Africa's digital divide. We don't just provide equipment—we build sustainable ecosystems where learners, teachers, and entire communities gain the digital skills essential for the 21st century economy.

Every year, over 60% of South African youth remain unemployed, largely due to lack of digital skills. Meanwhile, 70% of jobs now require ICT competency. Our program directly addresses this crisis by transforming non-functional computer labs into modern learning centers and equipping township youth with marketable skills.

Our Promise: Within 12 months of program implementation, we achieve a 75% increase in student digital proficiency and train 75% of educators in technology integration—creating a multiplier effect that benefits entire school communities.

Our 3-Phase Transformation Roadmap

Systematic, measurable, and sustainable impact

Phase 1: Foundation (Pilot Program)

Building Infrastructure & Skills | 6-12 Months

What We Do

We begin with comprehensive lab inspections, complete refurbishment to national ICT standards, and deployment of structured digital literacy training. Our pilot establishes the infrastructure, curriculum, and support systems that ensure sustainable success.

Infrastructure Development

  • Lab Assessment: Standardized evaluation of hardware, connectivity, and infrastructure using comprehensive inspection protocols
  • Complete Refurbishment: Modern computers, servers, high-speed internet, and secure networking equipment
  • Safety & Security: Proper electrical systems, security protocols, and equipment protection measures
  • Software Installation: Licensed operating systems, productivity suites, and educational software

Training Programs Launch

  • Student Training: Foundational digital literacy for 100+ learners per school
  • Teacher Development: Professional ICT integration workshops for educators
  • Lab Management: Training for lab technicians and administrators
  • Community Orientation: Engaging parents and community members

Phase 1 Impact Metrics

Success Rate: 100% of labs fully operational within timeline

Expanding Access & Impact

With infrastructure in place, we integrate Investhood IT's Learning Management System (LMS) to create a hybrid learning environment. Students access content both online and in-person, enabling continuous engagement, progress tracking, and scalable reach beyond physical classrooms.

Hybrid Learning Model

  • In-Lab Sessions: Hands-on practice with direct teacher support (3 hours/week)
  • Virtual Lessons: Live online classes for continued learning (2 hours/week)
  • Self-Paced Modules: Students work at their own pace on assignments
  • Mobile Labs: Extended access through portable computer units

Curriculum Expansion

  • Advanced Office Suite: Excel formulas, PowerPoint presentations, Word formatting
  • Internet & Research: Online safety, research methods, information literacy
  • Digital Communication: Email, virtual collaboration, professional presence
  • Creative Tools: Basic graphic design, video editing, multimedia creation

Community Extension

Labs remain open after school hours for community members, creating digital learning hubs that benefit entire neighborhoods. Weekend workshops target unemployed youth and parents, multiplying program impact.

Phase 3: Impact (Career Readiness)

Connecting Skills to Opportunities | 18-24 Months & Ongoing

From Learning to Earning

The final phase focuses on advanced skills development, industry certifications, and direct employment pathways. We partner with corporations to provide internships, mentorship, and career opportunities that transform digital literacy into economic empowerment.

Advanced Training Tracks

  • Web Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript fundamentals for junior developer roles
  • Digital Marketing: Social media management, SEO basics, content creation
  • Data Analysis: Excel data tools, basic analytics, report generation
  • IT Support: Hardware troubleshooting, software installation, help desk skills
  • Graphic Design: Adobe basics, visual communication, branding concepts

Industry Certifications

  • Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS): Recognized by employers worldwide
  • Google Workspace Certification: Cloud collaboration expertise
  • CompTIA IT Fundamentals: Entry-level IT credential
  • Digital Literacy Certificates: Custom program completion credentials

Employment Pathways

  • Internship Placements: 3-6 month internships with partner companies
  • Job Readiness Workshops: CV writing, interview skills, workplace etiquette
  • Mentorship Programs: Industry professionals guide graduates
  • Career Fairs: Direct connections to hiring companies
  • Freelance Training: Building independent income streams

Comprehensive Curriculum: Skills That Transform Lives

Five progressive learning modules designed for real-world success

1. Digital Foundations

Duration: 8 weeks | Hours: 24

Essential computer literacy: operating systems, file management, internet navigation, and online safety. Building confidence from day one.

Outcomes: Students can independently operate computers and navigate digital environments safely.

2. Productivity Tools

Duration: 12 weeks | Hours: 36

Mastering Microsoft Office Suite, Google Workspace, presentations, spreadsheets, and professional documentation. Skills that unlock academic and career opportunities.

Outcomes: Professional-quality documents, presentations, and spreadsheets ready for workplace use.

3. Digital Communication

Duration: 6 weeks | Hours: 18

Email etiquette, virtual collaboration, video conferencing, and professional online presence. Preparing learners for remote work and global connectivity.

Outcomes: Students communicate professionally across digital platforms and manage virtual teams.

4. Creative & Technical Skills

Duration: 10 weeks | Hours: 30

Introduction to coding, web design, digital media, and problem-solving. Unlocking creativity and innovation through technology.

Outcomes: Basic websites, digital graphics, and understanding of programming logic.

5. Certification & Beyond

Duration: 4 weeks | Hours: 12

Recognized certifications that validate skills and open doors. Connecting learners to further education, internships, and employment pathways.

Outcomes: Industry-recognized certificates and clear pathway to employment or further study.

Total Program

Duration: 40 weeks (1 academic year)

Total Hours: 120 contact hours + 80 hours self-paced learning

Completion Rate: 78% (industry standard: 45%)

Certification Rate: 92% of completers earn at least one credential
