About Digitize Kasi

Transforming township education through digital innovation and partnerships

Who We Are & What We Do

Digitize Kasi is a transformative non-profit initiative bridging the digital divide in South African township schools. Through strategic partnerships, modern infrastructure, and comprehensive training, we're empowering the next generation with essential digital skills.

We work directly with township schools to transform outdated computer labs into modern learning environments where students and teachers gain the skills to thrive in the 21st century.

Our Story: From Vision to Impact

How we're transforming digital education in South Africa

The Beginning

Founded by educators and tech professionals who witnessed firsthand the inequality in digital access. Township schools had computer labs, but they sat unused—broken equipment, no internet, no training. We knew we had to act.

The Partnership

We brought together Dream House Technologies, Investhood IT, and Kefilwe Tsela—each contributing unique expertise in infrastructure, digital learning platforms, and teacher training to create a comprehensive solution.

The Mission

Today, we're restoring computer labs, training thousands of learners and educators, and building sustainable digital ecosystems in Gauteng township schools—with plans to expand nationwide.

The Challenge: Why This Work Matters

Understanding the urgent digital divide in South African education

ICT Infrastructure Crisis

The majority of township schools lack functional computer labs or reliable network access. Outdated equipment, insufficient maintenance, and connectivity gaps leave thousands of learners without basic digital tools essential for modern education.

Impact: 70% of labs non-functional

Skills Deficit

A growing digital literacy gap severely limits employment opportunities for youth. Without foundational ICT skills, learners are unprepared for a technology-driven economy and struggle to compete in higher education and the job market.

Impact: 60% youth unemployment rate

Teacher Preparedness

Many educators lack training in technology integration. Without proper support and professional development, teachers cannot effectively leverage digital tools to enhance learning outcomes, perpetuating the cycle of digital exclusion.

Impact: Limited ICT training programs

Our Vision & Mission

The future we're building together

Our Vision

A digitally inclusive South Africa where every learner and educator in township schools has access to reliable technology, digital resources, and the skills required to thrive in the modern economy. We envision communities empowered through technology, where opportunity is determined by potential, not postal code.

Our Mission

To equip township schools with standardized computer labs, develop digital skills among learners and teachers, and foster innovation through accessible technology and training. We are committed to creating sustainable, scalable solutions that transform educational outcomes and open pathways to economic participation.

Our Core Goals

Measurable objectives driving our work forward

Infrastructure Excellence

Establish fully equipped computer labs that meet or exceed national ICT standards in every partner school.

Teacher Empowerment

Train 75% of educators in effectively integrating ICT into their teaching methods within the first year.

Student Success

Increase student digital proficiency by over 75% within the first year of program implementation.

Community Engagement

Involve local communities in program ownership, creating sustainable ecosystems of digital learning.

Scalable Impact

Develop replicable models that can expand from pilot schools to district-wide and nationwide implementation.

Innovation Hub

Transform schools into innovation centers where creativity, problem-solving, and entrepreneurship flourish.

Our Strategic Partners

Collaboration that drives transformation

Dream House Technologies

Infrastructure & Technology Partner

Manages server configuration, maintenance, and data storage for all participating labs. Develops proprietary applications for centralized administration and ensures robust security protocols. Dream House Technologies brings enterprise-level IT expertise to township school environments.

Investhood IT

Learning Management System Partner

Provides the comprehensive LMS platform that powers our digital curriculum. Hosts courses, facilitates live virtual sessions, manages assessments, and issues certifications. Their platform enables us to reach thousands of learners simultaneously while tracking individual progress.

Kefilwe Tsela

Training & Facilitation Partner

Leads teacher and student training sessions, providing curriculum content aligned with South African digital literacy standards. Designs practical lessons that enable learners to apply skills in real-world contexts, ensuring educational quality and cultural relevance.
