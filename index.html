The movement to accelerate educational Equality in under served communities

Digitize kasi aims to bridge the digital divide in South Africa's educational system. We work directly with local facilities to provide learners with modern learning environments where they can gain the practical skills for productivity and problem solving.

Every learner deserves access to quality digital education, regardless of their zip code. Through strategic partnerships, cutting-edge technology, and community-driven implementation, we're making this vision a reality across Gauteng and beyond.

Our Mission

We aim to accelerate digital progress in underserved communities

Educational Equality

The gap between resourced and under-resourced schools continues to widen. Students without digital access fall further behind, limiting their academic potential and future opportunities.

Economic Opportunity

Digital skills are no longer optional—they're essential. Over 70% of jobs now require basic ICT competency. Without these skills, township youth face systematic exclusion from meaningful employment.

Social Justice

Access to technology is a rights issue. Every child deserves the tools to reach their full potential. We're working to ensure zip codes don't determine futures

Competetiveness

South Africa's youth must compete globally. Digital literacy isn't just about using technology—it's about problem-solving, creativity , and innovation in an interconnected world.

Productivity

Practical digital skills empower learners with organization abilities that foster academic excellence and transcends to coorpearate productivity.

Innovation

The application of practical digital skills to creative and innovative minds breeds a generation of capable problem solvers and it empowers entrepreneural spirit in communities
